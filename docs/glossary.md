# Glossary

## Offering

Something a participant brings into the Jam: an idea, story, question, spec, commitment, refusal, source, practice, sketch, prototype, or relationship.

## Quick Card

The five plain-language questions used before any technical template.

## Spec Fragment

A wrapped offering with enough context to understand purpose, permissions, boundaries, and possible fit with other work.

## Bundle

A group of fragments that may fit together for a shared build or witnessing attempt.

## Coordination Canoe

Provisional working name for the Creator Jam pattern where participant offerings, specs, commitments, refusals, reviews, and receipts are carried together into a selected build attempt.

This is an English working metaphor. It is not a cultural, ceremonial, or language claim. Do not replace it with a Coast Salish language term unless the right language and protocol authority explicitly approves that exact use.

## Waka

The name of Austin Wade Smith / RiverComputer's project: https://github.com/RiverComputer/waka_docs

In this kit, use `Waka` only when referring to that external project or a bridge/interop path with it. Do not use `Waka` as a name for our Creator Jam pattern.

## Frozen Handoff Packet

Plain build attempt instructions for a selected bundle. It names authorized inputs, non-inputs, acceptance criteria, refusal boundaries, and expected outputs.

## Receipt

A record of what happened. It is not approval, permission, authority, certification, or readiness for reuse.

## Witness Rollup

A readable summary of what was attempted, what passed, what was partial, what failed, what was refused, and what was not run.

## Review Required

A state meaning the material should not be used, displayed, bundled, summarized, or sent to AI until the named review route clears it.
