# Consent, Refusal, And Review

The Jam treats consent, refusal, withdrawal, and review requirements as first-class outputs.

## Consent

Consent is specific. A person can approve one use and refuse another.

Record separately:

- contribution received
- display approved
- AI use approved
- reuse approved
- attribution preference
- follow-up permission

## Refusal

Refusal is not a blocker to hide. It is information the group should respect.

Record:

- do not use
- not for display
- not for AI
- not for bundling
- not for reuse
- review required before any next step

## Review Required

If something is cultural, linguistic, ceremonial, Nation-specific, steward-held, participant-private, or otherwise authority-bound, record `review_required` and name the review route if the contributor wants to name it.

Do not include the protected content itself.

## Withdrawal

Participants can change their mind. A withdrawal should record what changed, what should stop, and whether a public tombstone note is wanted.
