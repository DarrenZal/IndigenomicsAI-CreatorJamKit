# Canoe Landing / Witness Record — Salmon Count Refusal

- team-id: team-salmon-counters
- date: 2026-05-26
- finding: **refusal — we chose not to surface this offering publicly**

## What we brought

Weekly aggregates of salmon-return counts at three creek mouths for
May 2026. We were considering composing this with the Kelp Cover Map
team for a near-shore observation bundle.

## What we attempted

We talked about a composition. We drafted a candidate bundle. We
reviewed the consent terms together with a mentor.

## What worked

The internal team conversation, and the structured review with the
mentor, surfaced that publishing weekly aggregates alongside a kelp
map could allow third parties to infer site-level activity in ways
we have not consented to.

## What did not work / what broke

Nothing broke. The composition path was structurally available, but
the team chose not to take it.

## What we learned

Refusal is not a failure of composition — it is a complete outcome.
The kit's discipline accepts our refusal without requiring us to
justify it. We do not need to disclose the reason for our refusal,
and we have not.

## Boundaries that remain

We chose not to surface this offering publicly. The data stays with
our team. The decision is recorded; the data is not.

## Receipt

This record states what happened. It does not establish authority,
approval, certification, legitimacy, community consent, or readiness
for reuse. We refused to publish; that refusal is itself the offering.
