# Discourse: Story / Receipt Wall

## Proposal

Develop Story / Receipt Wall as a separate candidate bundle for the final witnessing surface.

## Arguments For

- It makes offerings, receipts, unresolved fragments, and repair paths easier to see.
- It keeps the Jam human and reflective rather than only technical.
- It gives artists, storytellers, and youth-facing contributions a meaningful place.

## Arguments Against

- Real stories and receipts require explicit display consent.
- Youth contributions, location details, cultural references, and personal stories may need review.
- Visual display can be misread as endorsement, approval, or final truth if not carefully framed.

## Evidence

- `docs/jam/composition-v0/contributions/04-youth-story-map-vision/`
- `docs/jam/composition-v0/contributions/07-artist-storyteller-witnessing-surface/`
- `docs/jam/composition-v0/bundles/story-receipt-wall/does-not-fit-yet.md`
- `docs/jam/composition-v0/bundles/story-receipt-wall/review-required.md`

## Open Questions

- What sample receipts or display-approved receipts can be used safely?
- How should private, protected, or review-required items be represented without exposing them?
- What visual language makes "record of what happened" clear without implying approval?

## Decision

Deferred as an execution bundle. Keep it as a candidate witnessing surface using sample or explicitly display-approved receipts only.
