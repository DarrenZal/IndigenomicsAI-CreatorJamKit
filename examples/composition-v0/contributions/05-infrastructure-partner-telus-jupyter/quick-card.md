# Quick Card: Infrastructure Partner TELUS/Jupyter

## What are you offering?

Optional TELUS/Jupyter support for running a notebook or model-backed summary on public sample material.

## What do you hope it helps with?

Show that partner infrastructure can support the Jam without replacing the manual path.

## What should it not be used for?

Do not make TELUS/Jupyter a condition for success. Do not process protected or participant-private content.

## Is anything private, protected, or review-required?

Operational review is required for access, logging, model routing, and what data may be processed.

## Who should be credited or asked?

TELUS/Jupyter partner contact, Shawn/operator, facilitator, and any contributor whose public material is used.
