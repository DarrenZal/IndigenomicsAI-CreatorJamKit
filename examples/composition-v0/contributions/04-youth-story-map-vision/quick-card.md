# Quick Card: Youth Story / Map Vision

## What are you offering?

A public story/map vision where young participants can place dreams, questions, and commitments on a simple map or wall.

## What do you hope it helps with?

Make the Jam feel imaginative and intergenerational, not only technical.

## What should it not be used for?

Do not extract personal stories, locations, cultural references, or family information without review and explicit permission.

## Is anything private, protected, or review-required?

Personal stories, precise locations, and cultural references are review-required. A generic public sample is allowed.

## Who should be credited or asked?

The youth contributor, guardian or facilitator where appropriate, and any person or place named.
