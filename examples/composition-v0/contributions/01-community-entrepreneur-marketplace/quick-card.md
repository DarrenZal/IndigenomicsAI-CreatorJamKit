# Quick Card: Community Entrepreneur Marketplace

## What are you offering?

A vision for a local Indigenous marketplace / offering directory where people can name what they offer, what they need, and what relationships or commitments they want to grow.

## What do you hope it helps with?

Help community entrepreneurs see each other, coordinate small commitments, and make reciprocity more visible without needing a full platform.

## What should it not be used for?

Do not turn people into rankings, scores, leads, or extractive marketing profiles.

## Is anything private, protected, or review-required?

Business names and offers need contributor approval before display. Contact details are private unless explicitly shared.

## Who should be credited or asked?

The entrepreneur who made the offer, any named business, and anyone whose relationship or story is referenced.
