# Artifact

## Working Title

Local Indigenous Marketplace / Offering Directory

## Offering

A plain-language directory card:

- name or pseudonym
- offering
- commitment available now
- what kind of collaboration is welcome
- who should be asked before public display

## Example Public Card

```text
I can contribute food for one community learning session and introduce two local entrepreneurs who may want to join future offering circles.
```

## Possible Connection

This fits the Open Kit Collective Demo as a public commitment example if the contributor approves the display wording.
