# Quick Card: Technical Builder Commitment Receipts

## What are you offering?

A small prototype path that shows a public commitment becoming a visible tuple and a receipt-shaped record.

## What do you hope it helps with?

Make the Jam concrete: participants can see how a commitment becomes visible without needing production infrastructure.

## What should it not be used for?

Do not present it as a production pool, authority decision, monitoring program, or certification process.

## Is anything private, protected, or review-required?

Use only public sample commitments or display-approved participant commitments.

## Who should be credited or asked?

The builder, the contributor whose commitment is shown, and the maintainer of the Open Kit demo.
