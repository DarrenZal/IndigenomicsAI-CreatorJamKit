# Artifact

## Working Title

Commitment Receipts Prototype

## Offering

A browser-based demo pattern:

1. Participant names a public commitment.
2. The demo shows a commitment tuple.
3. The demo emits a v0.2-shaped receipt.
4. A reviewer checks required receipt fields.
5. A witness rollup records what happened.
