# Quick Card: Data Steward Consent / Withdrawal

## What are you offering?

Rules for consent, attribution, withdrawal, tombstoning, and display approval.

## What do you hope it helps with?

Make sure participants can change their mind, be credited correctly, and prevent reuse that was not approved.

## What should it not be used for?

Do not turn consent into a one-time checkbox or assume a receipt gives permission forever.

## Is anything private, protected, or review-required?

Consent state and attribution preferences may be private. Public display requires explicit approval.

## Who should be credited or asked?

The contributor, the data steward/operator, and any steward or authority named by the contributor.
