# Artifact

## Working Title

Consent, Attribution, Withdrawal Rules

## Rules

- Every contribution has a named permission state.
- Display consent is separate from contribution consent.
- AI-use approval must be specific to the material and use.
- Withdrawal creates a tombstone or update note, not silent deletion from memory.
- Receipts can link to later corrections, withdrawals, or superseding receipts.

## Possible Connection

This is core to Consent / Review Desk and a required boundary for Story / Receipt Wall.
