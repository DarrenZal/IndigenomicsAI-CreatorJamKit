# Artifact

## Working Title

Collective Bundle "How We Know It Worked" Checklist

## Checklist

- Participants can explain what they offered.
- Facilitators can explain why each offering fits, does not fit yet, or needs review.
- Permissions and display boundaries are visible.
- The selected bundle has build attempt instructions.
- The builder/reviewer/witness loop records pass, partial, fail, refused, and not-run states.
- Receipts are explained as records, not approval or authority.
