# Quick Card: Researcher / Evaluator Criteria

## What are you offering?

A "how we know it worked" checklist for collective bundle attempts.

## What do you hope it helps with?

Keep the Jam accountable to clarity, consent, fit, and honest failure reporting rather than only technical success.

## What should it not be used for?

Do not turn it into a grading system for participants or a claim that a bundle is legitimate.

## Is anything private, protected, or review-required?

No protected content in the checklist itself.

## Who should be credited or asked?

The evaluator/researcher and any participants whose feedback shaped the criteria.
