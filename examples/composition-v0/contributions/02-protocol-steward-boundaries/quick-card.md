# Quick Card: Protocol Steward Boundaries

## What are you offering?

A refusal and review boundary for any cultural, protocol-bound, language, story, or Nation-specific material.

## What do you hope it helps with?

Make stop conditions visible and respected before any offering is bundled, displayed, or sent to AI.

## What should it not be used for?

Do not use this note to infer, summarize, translate, validate, or check protected material.

## Is anything private, protected, or review-required?

Yes. The protected content itself is not included. Only the boundary can be recorded.

## Who should be credited or asked?

The contributor-named authority, steward, community, Nation, or review route. Do not assume a generic reviewer can approve use.
