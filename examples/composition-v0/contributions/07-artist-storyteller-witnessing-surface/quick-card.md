# Quick Card: Artist / Storyteller Witnessing Surface

## What are you offering?

A public witnessing surface that makes selected offerings, receipts, and unresolved pieces easier to see and remember.

## What do you hope it helps with?

Keep the final moment human, visual, and reflective instead of only technical.

## What should it not be used for?

Do not make display feel like endorsement, approval, or final truth.

## Is anything private, protected, or review-required?

Real participant receipts require display consent. Use sample or explicitly display-approved receipts only.

## Who should be credited or asked?

The artist/storyteller, receipt contributors, and anyone named on the witnessing surface.
