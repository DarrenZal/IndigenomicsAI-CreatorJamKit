# Jam Offering Integration Report - Composition v0

Status: simulated manual-first report

## Purpose

This report tests whether many heterogeneous offerings can become candidate collective bundles without forcing one mega-spec.

The simulation uses eight fake contributors. No protected, cultural, protocol-bound, Nation-specific, participant-private, or authority-bound content is included.

## Candidate Bundles

| Bundle | Status | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Open Kit Collective Demo | selected for build attempt | Safest first collective bundle. Uses public commitments, sample receipts, clear refusal boundaries, and optional TELUS harness. |
| Consent / Review Desk | active, not execution bundle | Required review surface for protected material, refusal, withdrawal, and display consent. |
| Story / Receipt Wall | deferred candidate | Strong final witnessing surface, but real receipts and stories require explicit display consent. |

## What Fits Into Open Kit Collective Demo

- Public commitment from the community entrepreneur, after wording approval.
- Commitment receipt prototype from the technical builder.
- Evaluator checklist.
- Consent and refusal boundaries as constraints.
- TELUS/Jupyter as optional notes-only support.

## What Does Not Fit Yet

- Youth story/map content beyond a generic sample card.
- Real receipt wall display.
- Any protected, protocol-bound, cultural, language, or Nation-specific material.
- Private contact details or private consent notes.

## Review Queue

| Item | Review Route | Needed Before |

| Protocol-bound material | Contributor-named authority or steward | Any use beyond boundary note |

| Real receipts on Story / Receipt Wall | Receipt contributors / data steward | Public projection |

## Fit Decisions

| Decision | Offering | Result | Reason |
|---|---|---|---|
| include | Marketplace public commitment | include with display approval | Useful public commitment for Open Kit. |
| include | Commitment receipts prototype | include | Existing Open Kit path is already validated. |
| include | Evaluator criteria | include | Improves participant clarity. |
| constrain | Protocol boundary | apply to all bundles | Stops protected material from entering AI or public display. |
| constrain | Consent/withdrawal rules | apply to all bundles | Required for attribution and reuse discipline. |
| fork | Youth story/map vision | Story / Receipt Wall | Needs display/review discipline before use. |
| fork | Artist witnessing surface | Story / Receipt Wall | Useful but display consent must come first. |
| fork | TELUS/Jupyter support | optional Open Kit support | Not a condition of success. |

## Selected Bundle

Selected for execution: `bundles/open-kit-collective-demo/`

Reason: it can safely show the collective composition pattern using public sample material and explicit refusal boundaries.

## Final Witnessing Surface

The final surface should show:

- selected bundle
- included offerings
- review-required offerings
- does-not-fit-yet offerings
- what the builder/reviewer/witness attempt recorded
- what remains for repair or later work

Receipts record what happened. They do not establish legitimacy, authority, certification, or readiness for reuse.
