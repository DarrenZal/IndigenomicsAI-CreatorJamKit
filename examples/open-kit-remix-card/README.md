# Open Kit Remix Card

Use this to adapt the public ecological Open Kit example to another public or explicitly approved domain.

## Questions

1. What is your public sample domain?
2. What commitment could be made visible?
3. What should not be used?
4. Is anything private, protected, or review-required?
5. Who should be credited or asked?
6. What would a small receipt record?
7. What would show that the attempt worked?

## Boundary

Do not use protected, cultural, linguistic, ceremonial, Nation-specific, participant-private, or authority-bound content as sample material.
